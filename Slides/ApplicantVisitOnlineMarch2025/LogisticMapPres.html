General form for a recurrence relation

\[u_{n+1}=r u_n+b\]

Convergence

The solutions converges if \(|r|<1\).

A limit

As \(n\rightarrow \infty\), \(u_n\rightarrow \frac{b}{1-r}\).

The limiting value is an example of a fixed point.
